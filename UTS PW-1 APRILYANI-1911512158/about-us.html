ABOUT US

PT Enera Teknika Utama is a general trading company that established in Tangerang on January 2014.

We provide engineering, mechanical, electrical and industrial equipment to our customer, such as but not limited to hand tools, pruning tools, saw tools, measuring equipment, grinder, automotive equipment, cleaner equipment, valve, fittings, pneumatics, instrument equipment, etc.

We are an authorized distributor for Bluepoint and Bahco Tools from Snapon Group USA and Draper Tools from UK. We also have strong relation with many product principals from local and international product that makes us can offer very competitive price with the best quality.
